Mindcelium is an entity logic processing library that specializes in making chunk loading unnecessary. It does this by compressing the world save into a highly abstracted form that contains only the bare minimum needed for logical interaction with the world.

Block updates? Schedule them for when the chunk is loaded in or optionally load them in whenever extra CPU space is available.

Hordes spawning inside your base? Need your mastermind entity (like Spore's Hivemind) to track players that are hundreds or thousands of blocks away in a fair manner? No problem. Move mobs across large swaths of unloaded land using advanced continental-level pathfinding tools.
